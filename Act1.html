The Happy Prince

Oscar Wilde

The Happy Prince by Oscar Wilde is a tale with multiple lessons.
From one side it is the critic of the society that can be cruel and heartless and on the other side, it is about the compassion towards human troubles.
The Happy Prince is a contemporary fairytale whose plot is surreal and it is placed into a modern society with real problems.
Oscar Wild tried to state in a simple way the virtues and flaws of a person in all of his fairy tales and tried to criticize the society which is insensible.
