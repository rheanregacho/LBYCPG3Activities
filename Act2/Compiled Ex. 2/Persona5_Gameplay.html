Persona 5 takes place within modern-day
Tokyo City. Wherein students, like the
Protagonist, can live like a normal
person. One of the gameplay simulates
a day-to-day life of a student.		 Metaverse, parallel space that is filled with
Shadows, are to be conquered by the Persona
Users. Approaching the Metaverse will lead
to encounters with Shadows resulting into a
turn-based fight that pits the characters'
skills and powers against the forces of evil.		 Characters' skills and powers are directly
connected to the bond that they hold with
other people. One essential aspect of the game
is to develop friendship levels that will
improve the fighting capacity
of characters inside the Metaverse.
