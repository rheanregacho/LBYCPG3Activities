How To Play



Welcome to the Wild Hunt!


The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt is an interactive game that allows you to traverse its world. Be curious and focus on the small details to achieve your goal - find the Child of Prophecy who can alter the shape of the world. Be smart in making your choices, 36 possible endings await you. This game is not about the scores, but make sure you have the best ending. Lastly, enjoy! Immerse yourself in the character of Geralt of Rivia, the strongest monster slayer!

Explore the World of the Witcher!


Call your trusty horse, load it with trophies taken from the massive beasts you hunt down or gallop it into combat. Go somewhere where no horse can follow, deep into caves, dive beneath the waves to gather sea herbs and search forgotten shipwrecks for treasures. Even sail with your own boat - all these possible as you explore their world!


Meet the Inhabitants


In search for information, make unsavory deals with criminals, attend balls with high-born and listen to the tales of war regufees. Interact with animals both hostile and benign and witness a true ecosystem. Be their apex predator!

Engage in Activities


Take a break from monster hunting just to join in the fun or to win some coin to further your goals. Ttry your hand at gwent, a deep and rewarding game-within-a-game. Invented by dwarves and played for fun or money, this simple yet addictive pastime will have you sitting down for match after match and constantly thinking about how to bolster your deck.

Learn your Mutations and Skillls


It is important to know your skills. Witchers' mutations endow them with incredible speed, reflexes, and strength and enhance their senses. The Witcher's Signs are also powerful combat spells that take various forms from protection and more. Geralt's wolf medallion hums to alert him of nearby ,agic and monsters. Increase your potency and develop additional effects to make yourself stronger!

Pick your Weapon!


Geralt has an extensive arsenal of weapons! From swords and axes to crossbows and unique gear to help deal with death. His armors absorb both physical and magical attacks. Thanks to his training in alchemy, Geralt can gather rare herbs along his path and use them to brew special, highly toxic concoctions.

Hunt Monsters!


Eliminate creatures that pose a threat to society. Monsters come in all shapes and sizes, and while some are members of broader species, others are one-of-a-kind. Embark on special quests to learn about their fighting style and behavior.

Recognize your Allies and Enemies


The enemy of your enemy is your friend. Geralt is a professional, and if it helps get the job done, he’ll accept the aid of scoundrels and brutes, as well as sorceresses and jolly dwarves. In his line of work, he can also become a target. Those with different agendas send their spies, assassins, murderers and thugs after him. You’re on your own -- and in for a tough fight.
